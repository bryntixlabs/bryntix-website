# Bryntix Labs: Setup & Scaling Framework

Welcome to the operational blueprint for **Bryntix Labs**, a specialized tech consultancy and agency specializing in Web Development, Mobile App Development, UI/UX Design, and AI Solutions based in India.

This workspace contains a four-part comprehensive playbook to guide your business registration, engineering pipelines, marketing strategies, and execution checklists.

---

## 📂 Playbook Directory

### ⚖️ 1. [Business & Legal Setup](file:///C:/Users/sahaj/Documents/antigravity/blissful-turing/company-setup/business_and_legal.md)
Contains the regulatory, legal, and structural requirements for launching a tech firm in India:
- **Business Registration**: Comparison between LLP and Pvt. Ltd. and step-by-step registration.
- **GST & Exports**: Detailed guidelines on GSTR returns and zero-rated export of services under LUT.
- **Verification & Infrastructure**: Setting up DUNS Numbers, DNS configurations (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), and Google Workspace.
- **Taxation & Corporate Profiles**: Indian direct and indirect tax rates, TDS rules, and payment gateways.
- **Legal Protection**: Frameworks for Privacy Policies, Terms & Conditions, and Milestone-based Refund Policies.

### 🛠️ 2. [Operations & Engineering Guide](file:///C:/Users/sahaj/Documents/antigravity/blissful-turing/company-setup/operations_and_engineering.md)
Establishes execution pipelines and developer practices to match elite engineering cultures:
- **IAM & Permissions**: Strict role mapping following the Principle of Least Privilege (PoLP).
- **Client Onboarding**: A step-by-step pipeline for kickoffs, security credential collection, and Slack channel setups.
- **Billing & CRM Integration**: Standard invoicing workflows and CRM setup (HubSpot, Linear).
- **Trunk-Based Git Strategy**: Short-lived feature branches, feature flags, and deployment environments.
- **CI/CD Pipeline Configurations**: Vercel (web), Codemagic (mobile), and GitHub Actions (backend/AI services).
- **Security & Docs**: API key rotations, secret management (Doppler), OWASP Top 10, C4 model, and API documentation standards.
- **Company Shared Directory**: Logical Google Drive structure template and a sample SOP for hotfixes.

### 🚀 3. [Marketing & Growth Strategy](file:///C:/Users/sahaj/Documents/antigravity/blissful-turing/company-setup/marketing_and_growth.md)
A blueprint to position Bryntix Labs as a premium consultancy and win high-ticket international clients:
- **Visual Branding**: Curated corporate color palettes (Obsidian, Space Blue, Teal, Purple) and typographic guidelines.
- **B2B Inbound & Outbound Playbooks**: Value-driven LinkedIn content calendars, Loom audits, and productized consulting funnels.
- **Search Engine Optimization**: Core web vitals metrics, schema markup, and high-value content clusters (AI & Mobile apps).
- **High-Converting Case Studies**: Design architecture for story-driven portfolios.
- **3-Year Scaling Roadmap**: Moving from pure agency services to productized workflows and proprietary AI SaaS modules.
- **Risk Mitigation & Mistakes**: Managing currency risk (EEFC accounts), client concentration, scope creep, and pricing pitfalls.

### 🧰 4. [Execution Toolkit & Quickstart Kit](file:///C:/Users/sahaj/Documents/antigravity/blissful-turing/company-setup/execution_toolkit.md)
Actionable files and sheets to immediately launch operations:
- **Phase-by-Phase Checklist**: Breakdowns from Day 1 to Day 45.
- **Indian Cost Sheet**: Government incorporation fees, stamp duty, DSCs, domains, and professional CA charges.
- **Recommended SaaS Stack**: A visual map of CRM, Billing, Git, hosting, and operations software.
- **30-60-90 Day Roadmap**: Concrete, time-bound goals for building traction.
- **Business FAQs**: Quick answers regarding physical offices, GST refunds, e-FIRC collection, and AI API privacy.
